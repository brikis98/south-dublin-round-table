What is Round Table?

Round Table is an organization for men under the age of 45, dedicated to building lasting friendships and making a positive impact in our communities.

The South Dublin Round Table was chartered in late 2025 and is one of the newest additions to the global Round Table family, which has clubs in over 60 countries. We meet up every other Thursday night to do cool activities, grab a beer, volunteer, and hang out.

Build friendships

Connect with like-minded young men and forge lifelong friendships through regular social gatherings and shared experiences.

Share adventures

Explore Ireland and travel the world together through organized trips, outdoor activities, and unforgettable adventures.

Give back

Make a real difference in South Dublin through meaningful community service projects and charitable initiatives.
