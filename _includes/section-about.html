What is Round Table?

Round Table is an international organization for young men under the age of 45, dedicated to building lasting friendships and making a positive impact in our communities.

The South Dublin Round Table was chartered in late 2025 and is one of the newest additions to the global Round Table family. We're excited to build a strong community here in South Dublin!

Part of a Global Movement

South Dublin Round Table 1256 is part of the wider Round Table family, built around friendship, personal growth, and service.

Rechartered in 2025

We are a new chapter in South Dublin with regular events, new members joining, and a strong focus on local impact.
